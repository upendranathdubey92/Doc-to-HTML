How long does it take to develop a website or web application?
Simple websites typically take 4-6 weeks, medium complexity applications take 2-3 months, and enterprise-grade platforms may require 4-6 months. Timeline depends on project scope, number of features, integrations, and your team's availability for feedback cycles.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Can you integrate with our existing systems?
Absolutely. We have extensive experience integrating with CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), ERPs (SAP, Oracle), payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal), and custom APIs. We'll assess your systems during discovery and plan the integration strategy.

Yes, we sign NDAs before any project discussions and include IP assignment clauses in our contracts to ensure all code and designs belong to you upon final payment.

TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS:
✅ Keep section markers on their own lines
✅ Use consistent formatting throughout
✅ Don't add extra spaces in section marker names
✅ Provide meaningful content (avoid "Lorem ipsum")
✅ Test with small documents first
✅ Review generated HTML before using in production

❌ Don't use old-style section names (they won't work)
❌ Don't forget END markers
❌ Don't add Word comments (they'll be filtered out but avoid them)
❌ Don't mix MCB sections with SOI sections in same document
